## Slide 4 — Secure Login Flow

**Message:** Flexible sign-in for visitors, members, and administrators.

**Bullets**
- Non-members sign in with a one-time code sent to email
- ICAI members can connect to Self-Service Portal (SSP) in production
- Administrators use a separate secure login for the CMS
- After authentication, the same reader experience is available
